Getting to Yes

by Daniel Rosen
The Story of Our Proposal

Three years ago, I walked into District (a wine bar in SF) expecting nothing more than some casual industry chat with a new acquaintance...but the woman I met turned out to be the best surprise of my life. From the moment I met Tammy, I could tell she was independent, fearless and strong-willed. Over time (yes, it took a while), Tammy revealed how kind, generous and loving she is as well.

Ours is a love built both on commonality and individuality - the things we share, and the differences we respect. As weeks turned into months, we traded in our astronomical Uber bills for a couple of keys to the same apartment. Once again, Tammy surprised me with her domestic aptitudes (that girl can COOK!)...certainly not what I was expecting from a kickass entrepreneur and world record-holding kiteboarder!

When we first discussed Burning Man, I had no idea what to expect. Imagine...a week in the desert, with no running water, no electricity...no phone service! I wasn't prepared for how amazing an experience it would turn out to be. In fact, the only thing I knew for certain when I boarded the RV was that I had an important question to ask Tammy...but I didn't know exactly when.

A couple of days into the festival, after a long night of dancing and celebrating, we decided to watch the sunrise from a special spot in the middle of the playa (AKA the desert in the middle of the 'city'). As the sun crept above the distant hills and lit the sky with orange, I looked at Tammy and realized it was time.

Tammy - thank you for being in my life. I want to spend the rest of it with you...I love you.
